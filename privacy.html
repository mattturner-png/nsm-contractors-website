Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Who We Are

NSM Group Ltd (trading as NSM Contractors) provides property maintenance and renovation services across South East England. Our contact email is hello@nsmcontractors.com and our phone number is 07493 170 661.

What Information We Collect

When you submit an enquiry through our website, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address
  • Details of your project or enquiry

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your enquiry and provide you with a quote for our services. We do not use your data for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

How We Store Your Information

Enquiry submissions are processed through Netlify Forms and delivered to us by email. We do not store your personal data on any third-party databases beyond what is necessary to respond to your enquiry.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data is used only by NSM Contractors to respond to your enquiry.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@nsmcontractors.com.

Cookies

Our website does not use tracking cookies. The Instagram feed widget (Behold) may set cookies — please refer to Behold's privacy policy for details.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at hello@nsmcontractors.com or call 07493 170 661.
